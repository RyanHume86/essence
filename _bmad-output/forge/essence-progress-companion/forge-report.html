The Forge · Idea Pressure-Test · Verdict of Record

Essence

The Visible-Progress Companion

Method: Prove-or-Kill  •  Status: Complete  •  Sealed 2026-06-24

Hardened

Conditionally — hardened under a reframed goal.

The idea survived the interrogation, but only after the founder reframed the goal mid-session: from a scalable, mass-market, monetized business to a niche, cost-covering companion for a sliver. The stamp holds. The original ambition does not.

The Committed Idea

A visible-progress companion you create yourself — a calm, no-shame character laid over real identity-level goals, built for the sliver, not the masses; monetized only to cover costs, designed v1 for the builder moving a novel or a business, alienated by clinical trackers and cutesy-gacha pets alike.

Locked

What survived

load-bearing · carried forward
  • The core is the feeling — warmth + visible progress. Not the features, not the AI; task-AI demoted to an optional add-on.
  • The moat is the feeling done with restraint — genuine no-shame discipline tied to real identity-level goals: the one thing Finch cannot copy without ceasing to be Finch.
  • Goal (B): niche, cost-covering — cover sync/server cost for a real, paying sliver; not a paywall, not a business engine.
  • Primary persona: the builder — someone moving a novel or a business, alienated by clinical minimalists and cutesy pets both.
  • Differentiation is evidence-based — grounded in documented Finch complaints, not founder taste.
Killed

What did not survive

struck · with cause
  • Goal (A): scalable, mass-market. A solo founder cannot out-execute a 10M-user funded incumbent on its own turf; the empty middle is real at scale.
  • The streak / statistics page. It contradicts the no-shame rule — it is the guilt engine. Reached for to match Finch's analytics; founder conceded the oversight.
  • AI as the basis of the product. Task-AI is commoditized and giant-owned, and per-call cost on free users breaks the free-core economics.
  • “Warmth alone retains.” The character habituates; by week six the warmth is wallpaper. Retention needs the win-moment loop, not novelty.
Cracks That Hold

Conditions before building

unresolved · load it before you scale
  • Get a real payment signal. From more than one unbiased builder. “Pays for Candy Crush” is a casual-game wallet, not proven willingness to pay for a calm companion-tracker.
  • Build the win-moment loop, prove it on a stranger. Show the feeling lands on a stranger-builder — not just on the founder.
  • Hold scope. Resist the nine-feature creep. The moat is the feeling + no-shame restraint tied to real goals, never the feature list.

The Pivotal Move

The idea did not survive on its merits as first stated. It survived a single, honest pivot: the founder reframing the goal from A — scalable, mass-market, monetized — to B: a niche, cost-covering companion for a sliver. The empty-middle kill dissolved under B, and Priya stood down. The reframe is what saved it. The conditions are what keep it honest.
