# Forged Idea — Akha

**Verdict: HARDENED (conditional).** Survived prove-or-kill — but only after the goal was reframed from "scalable mass-market business" to "niche, cost-covering companion for a sliver."

## The idea (committed form)
A **visible-progress companion you create yourself** — a habit/task tracker overlaid with a relatable, no-shame emotional character. **For a sliver, not the masses.** Monetization exists to **cover costs, not to gate the experience.** Design v1 for **the builder**.

## Locked (survived the forge)
- **Core = the feeling** (warmth + visible progress), *not* features and *not* AI. Task-AI is an optional add-on at most.
- **Moat = the feeling delivered with taste + genuine no-shame restraint, tied to real identity-level goals** (your novel/business/body) — the one thing Finch structurally can't copy without ceasing to be Finch.
- **Goal = (B) niche / cost-covering**, explicitly not mass-market. The "empty middle" is the *thesis*, not a flaw, once you stop needing scale.
- **Primary persona = the builder** — moving real goals, alienated by both clinical trackers (Things/TickTick) and cutesy-gacha pets (Finch). (Not the retiree — a different product.)
- **Differentiation is evidence-based**, grounded in documented Finch complaints (not founder taste): shallow analytics, no focus/Pomodoro, no real data export (lock-in), slow completion loop, 12h pet wait, goal-order bugs, gacha/feature creep, divisive childish tone, device-local data loss, residual guilt despite "shame-free."

## Killed (and why)
- **"Scalable, many people" (goal A)** — as a mass product it's a kill: can't out-execute a 10M-user incumbent solo, and the empty middle is real at scale.
- **A streak / statistics page** — contradicts the one brand rule (no streaks, no shame); it *is* the guilt engine. (Founder conceded.)
- **AI assistant as the basis** — most commoditized, giant-owned capability; breaks free-core economics; was a reflex grab under attack.
- **"Warmth alone retains"** — habituates by week six; the warm-character-you-grow already exists at scale (Finch/Habitica).

## Conditions before building (the cracks that still hold)
1. **Get a real payment signal** from >1 *unbiased* builder. "Pays for Candy Crush" ≠ pays for a calm companion-tracker (different wallet); current n is 2, one of them the requester.
2. **Build the win-moment loop and prove the feeling lands on a stranger-builder** — the whole thesis is entangled with one 30-second moment.
3. **Hold scope.** The moat is the feeling + restraint, not the 9-feature list. Resist becoming the feature-creep you diagnosed in Finch.
