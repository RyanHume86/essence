- (technique) started Six Thinking Hats
- (insight by user) General to-do apps get used a couple sessions then overlooked; the real need is to be more engaging / retained
- (insight by coach) Retention cliff is structural: to-do apps are transactional (open w/ task, leave when done); winners add a reason to return even with nothing urgent
- (insight by user) Drifts away precisely when in FLOW: caught up in a task, it flows into the next without needing to look at the app — the disengagement moment IS success
- (insight by coach) Reframe: the app competes with flow itself; any engagement that requires opening the app interrupts flow = self-defeating. Win may be living at the SEAMS between tasks (ambient/voice/widget), not pulling the user in
- (technique) switched to Red Hat (feelings)
- (idea by user) At the seam between tasks, the wanted feeling is: 'I've achieved something' — a sense of accomplishment
- (idea by coach) Two flavors of achievement: micro-hit (checked a box) vs macro-meaning (the day added up toward something I care about). Seam could show the DENT made in a bigger goal, not just a checkmark
- (decision by user) CORE: achievement = watching something I care about VISIBLY MOVE (macro-meaning), not the checkbox tick — that's what would bring me back tomorrow
- (insight by coach) THESIS seed: Akha is not a to-do list but a visible-progress engine — tasks are fuel that move the things you care about, and you feel them move at the seams
- (technique) switched to Green Hat (new ideas / mechanics)
- (idea by user) At the seam: a smile, a 'well done', something inspiring and heartwarming
- (idea by coach) Implies Akha has a VOICE/personality: a warm companion in your corner (not a taskmaster). Delivered ambiently (earbud word/haptic/one line) so flow isn't broken; praise must be EARNED & TRUE (tie to real movement) or it becomes hollow Clippy
- (technique) wrapped Six Thinking Hats; started Mind Mapping
- (idea by user) Companion vibe = a warm friend who's PLEASED for you, not hyped. Hype feels patronizing.
- (insight by coach) 'Pleased not hyped' = ownable differentiator: every habit app is LOUD/hyped; Akha is restrained. Rarity + specific knowing acknowledgment makes praise land without condescending; implies companion has MEMORY of what you're working toward
- (idea by user) Return-moment words: 'It's so good to see you! Welcome back!' — glad, zero guilt
- (insight by coach) Anti-shame return embodied: no 'where were you', progress preserved (plant napped not died), rekindles the WHY. This warmth IS the retention/monetization engine — you pay to keep a companion who knows you, not for a to-do app
- (technique) Mind Map: pivot to 'Where money comes in' branch
- (decision by user) Monetization model: app free with ALL basic features; charge for integrations / cross-platform sync (Google Calendar, Notion, etc.)
- (insight by coach) Principle: monetize the PLUMBING, never the WARMTH — paywalling the companion's love poisons it (conditional warmth isn't warmth); user's instinct protects the soul. Reframe paid tier as 'your companion everywhere' (continuity across your life), not raw sync. Risk: integrations-only may be a thin payer base
- (idea by user) Paid 'continuity' tier = backup (never lose your garden), cross-device sync, sync with other apps
- (technique) wrapped Mind Mapping; started Time Horizon Ladder (1yr/10yr/100yr)
- (idea by user) Year-1 vision: word-of-mouth spreads like wildfire, as many people as possible using it to its fullest; founder 'would LIKE to say yes' he'd still open it daily (note the hesitation)
- (insight by coach) The hesitation is the whole ballgame: if the founder isn't sure he'd open it daily, that's the retention cliff. Wildfire word-of-mouth can't be manufactured — it's downstream of genuine daily pull. Crack 'what makes ME open it unhesitatingly' and virality follows
- (idea by user) Recommendation sharpened: 'I never feel pressured but I actually want to do my tasks. I CREATED MY OWN CHARACTER that supports me and is genuinely glad when I achieve something.'
- (insight by coach) Big new idea: you CREATE/customize your own companion -> ownership = retention moat (can't ditch something you made) + onboarding hook (first 30s = make your character, instant ownership). North-star claim the product must earn: 'I actually want to do my tasks.'
- (technique) started One Feature Only (constraint) — facilitator's pick, to converge on the first build
- (decision by user) THE ONE FEATURE to build first = the win-moment loop (tick -> created character quietly pleased -> the Care visibly moves in real time -> sacred win)
- (idea by coach) Amplifiers of the win-loop: (1) warm signature SOUND/voice (half the feeling, ignored by all to-do apps); (2) hidden dependency — visible movement needs a CARE/goal to move so progress MEANS something; (3) proportional warmth scaled to size of win (anti-hype); (4) opt-in shareable milestone card = organic word-of-mouth engine
- (insight) SYNTHESIS — Thesis: Akha = a visible-progress companion you CREATE yourself; pleased not hyped; grows the Cares you choose; never shames; lives for one sacred 30s win. Free at heart, pay to carry it everywhere (plumbing, never warmth). North-star: earn 'I actually want to do my tasks.' BUILD FIRST: the win-moment loop (tick -> your character quietly pleased -> the Care moves in real time -> sacred win). Test the FEELING on yourself before breadth. Key cross-links: 'create your own character' = onboarding hook + retention moat + viral detail (one idea, 3 jobs); 'pleased not hyped' = same principle as 'monetize plumbing not warmth'; flow-drift insight = why it must live ambiently at the seam.
