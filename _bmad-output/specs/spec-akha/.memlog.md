- (capability) CAP-2 Capture & prioritize: user adds tasks any time, sets priority 1-5, decomposes into one-level subtasks (nudged not forced), edits/deletes — full list visible on the neutral Plan surface, creature passive (FR4-10, FR7). success: a task created on Plan with priority + subtasks persists and later surfaces in Focus by the ordering rule.
- (capability) CAP-3 One-task Focus: during doing, only the highest-ranked incomplete task shows; backlog off-screen; order = priority 5→1, then due (date-only) sooner-first, then added, with manual rearrange as a global override (FR11-12). success: with N>1 incomplete tasks, Focus renders exactly one (the top of effective order) and never the full backlog.
- (capability) CAP-4 Subtask reveal & complete: subtasks reveal two-at-a-time, exactly one active; completing the active one removes it, flows the next up, expands the following; fires the tier-1 reaction (gentle smile + brief line) (FR13-14). success: at most two subtasks shown, exactly one active; on complete the item vanishes and the next becomes active.
- (capability) CAP-5 Whole-task win: last subtask (or a no-subtask line) auto-completes the task — it dims but stays, sinks to bottom, the accumulation-only progress wheel grows, creature does a little dance (tier-2) (FR15-16, FR25). success: completing the final subtask auto-ticks the parent, dims+sinks it, advances the wheel with NO denominator/percentage/remaining.
- (capability) CAP-6 End the day on demand: explicit optional 'I'm done for today' (never auto-gated) sweeps the dimmed completed tasks into the Archive and the creature gives a deep slow sigh (tier-3) (FR19). success: the control is always present regardless of list state; tapping it moves completed tasks to Archive and plays the sigh; a late task can still be captured after.
- (capability) CAP-7 Calm carry-forward: incomplete tasks carry to the next day, gently highlighted 'still needs doing' — never 'overdue', never red/badge, never escalating by age OR volume, with a persistent non-colour label (FR21). success: a carried task shows the fixed calm treatment + 'still needs doing' label; treatment is identical whether 1 or many carry and never intensifies with age.
- (capability) CAP-8 Archive review: completed work is retained and viewable as a calm flat reverse-chronological 'what I did' history — never totals, per-day tiles, calendar grids, streaks, or any aggregate that re-encodes a scoreboard (FR22-23, FR26). success: Archive lists only completed tasks newest-first; no count/total/grid/streak element can be rendered by its structure.
- (capability) CAP-9 Personalize via Settings: name, colour scheme (7: 4 dark/3 light incl. white + colourblind-safe), creature, and a notifications toggle that always starts OFF are all editable post-onboarding (FR3, NFR7-9). success: changing the scheme in Settings re-skins every surface live via tokens; the push toggle defaults off and is only ever user-enabled.
